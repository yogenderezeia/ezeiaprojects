Security is paramount

Security & Privacy

This is something we take very seriously. After all, trust and transparency is everything when it comes to money matters.

We deploy the most advanced infrastructure which includes:

  • The safest browser encryption obtainable

  • The most secure servers and first-class data centres for data processing and storage

Protecting your privacy overrides everything and Kuvera will never share your personal data with any third party without your permission.

As part of our internal processes and procedures we observe the non-stop encryption, carry out daily back-ups, perform the auditing of log-ins, and monitor the secure storage of the data pertaining to your account.

Fraud and Account Protection

It’s advisable to always ensure your account and passwords are secure and safe from unauthorized activity. If you see any unauthorized activity in your account, report it immediately.

How Kuvera prevents fraudulent activities:

  • Identity and Account Ownership Verification

  • Automatic Logout

  • Continuous updating of Contact Information
