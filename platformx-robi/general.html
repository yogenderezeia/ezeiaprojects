Retirement

You may have something in mind when you invest – such as retirement, a big purchase or saving for someone’s education. However, it may be you just want to get better returns from your existing savings or pension pot. Or you may want to put a regular pension contribution to better use.

Whatever you want from your money Kuvera is the ideal place to invest. It’s low cost, easy to use and you can keep an eye on your money and its performance at any time. What people really like is the idea that they can take out or add money to their account at will – this puts you in control and everything is completely transparent.

Low investment contributions to get started

Furthermore, you don’t need to invest huge amounts of money with Kuvera. You can start with just $1,000 and contribute $100 per month thereafter. Alternatively you can invest £10,000 and leave it at that. This is ideal if you want to try us out and see what returns are possible – and then if you like what you see you can add more funds to your account.
