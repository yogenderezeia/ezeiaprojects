What we invest in

We believe long-term, intelligent asset allocation is the best investment approach. At Kuvera we keep things simple and transparent. We invest in different types of assets including bonds, cash, equities and property.

How we buy assets for you.

Once we've determined which assets should go into a portfolio, and in what proportions, we look to invest in them in the most efficient way possible. We generally prefer to use ETFS (Exchange-Traded Funds) because they’re easy to trade, low cost and aim to track the movement of market indices with a high degree of accuracy.
