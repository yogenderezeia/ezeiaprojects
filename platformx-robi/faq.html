Frequently Asked Questions

Important questions to consider…

Am I locked into the investment platform?

No. We believe that investing is a long game, however, with Kuvera you can withdraw your money whenever you like and there's no minimum investment period.

Is there a minimum investment?

Yes. The minimum investment is $1,000 for each fund you create, which allows us to create a diversified mixture of investments. For portfolios below $10,000, there’s a minimum monthly contributions of $100.

How much of my money is invested?

We put all your money to work which means no loss of performance and better returns.

Could I do this myself?

You could certainly try to do this on your own if you had the right experience, however, to rebalance and monitor your portfolio could result in high trading charges, not to mention time and hassle. Plus, because we can make trades in bulk across our clients, our costs are even lower.

Is this end-to-end investing?

Yes. By handling every part of the investment process we make things quick and easy for you. This means faster cash transfers and tighter security.

What is Kuvera’s track record?

We always publish our portfolio performance after fees and compared to our competitors so you can see exactly how we’re doing. Please remember that past performance is not a reliable indicator of future performance. View Kuvera’s performance figures for 2014.
