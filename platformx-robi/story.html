Hello, we're Kuvera

…and we're here to make it easy for people to invest (whilst keeping costs down too).

We started Kuvera in 2015 in Australia after talking about why financial services and particularly the investment world was so complicated and stuck in the past. Surely with online technology we could make a difference – make things easier, better… less intimidating - more approachable? So we dropped everything and got started.

Started with three questions

When we created Kuvera we asked ourselves these three simple questions:

  • Why do traditional investment institutions and fund managers charge so much for their services?

  • Why are people paying for expensive active investments when passive investments perform just as well?

  • How can we make investing simple and beneficial for everyone without the traditional high charges?

You see, it does not have to be as confusing and complicated as some financial experts would have you believe. And they don’t need to take a big slice of your wealth in high fees either – or maybe they do to fund their lavish lifestyles?

Kuvera was born

So for example, if a fund or wealth manager takes a 3% fee over 10 years for managing your capital – that’s 30% carved off your money. With Kuvera you’ll only pay a fraction of that cost and Yogi and our investment team will ensure our passive ETFs perform well by tracking indices closely.

So with that in mind, we thought with some clever technology and clever people behind the scenes we could offer something better and worthwhile. Kuvera was born.

The Times They Are a-Changin’

Famous words by Bob Dylan and with investing they couldn’t be truer. For a start, you have access to the internet with easier share dealing and online investment products and services. And we understand you want the convenience, transparency and affordability the internet brings – not the high costs and ‘smoke & mirrors’ that can often accompany traditional financial institutions.

A cheaper, more efficient way

However, partly as a result of this, the industry itself is changing with cheaper, more efficient ways of trading. For example, more trading is being undertaken using ETFs (Exchange Trading Fund) as they are cheaper, more transparent and more diversified than mutual funds.

And this is where Kuvera comes in – as we want to pass all those benefits onto you – so you have control over your investments using a website which is intuitive with a service which delivers, affordable and great value.

‘Citius est Melius’ (Quicker is Better).

This kind of sums up our thinking. We like to keep things simple and nimble so we can react swiftly and keep costs down. The modern world with its ever improving technology dictates that you can’t stand still for long so we like to combine investment wisdom with agility.

We believe this means you get a great service, a portfolio that performs and growing capital over time without paying over the odds. Simple.
